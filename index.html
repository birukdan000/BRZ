    Peyton Manning was good. Manning was really good. He is there with the greatest quarterbacks of all time. Manning was drafted first overall, in the first round, by the Indianapolis Colts, in 1998. His rookie season wasn't the most dominant, because he threw 26 touchdowns, but at the same time 28 interceptions. His most dominant season was with the Denver Broncos, where he was traded in 2012. In 2013 he threw for 55 touchdowns beating his own record of 49 which he set in 2004. Manning played a total of 18 seasons, and won 2 superbowls.
